Common Questions

What are early signs of autism? ＋
Common indicators can include differences in social interactions, delayed or specialized language patterns, repetitive behaviors, or heightened reactions to sensory details. Every child's balance is entirely unique.
How can I build a sensory-friendly routine? ＋
Creating predictability through clear schedules, integrating visual charts, limiting harsh sound elements, and planning quiet environments for sensory downtime are great ways to begin establishing a routine.
